⚙️ How It Works

Two Random Forest models work together — one predicts the best crop, the other estimates yield production.

1

Enter Farm Data

Provide your state, district, season, temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and area.

2

Feature Engineering

25 features are computed: log transforms, climate interactions, regional yield statistics, and encoded location data.

3

Crop Prediction

A Random Forest Classifier (85%+ accuracy) predicts the best crop and top 3 alternatives with confidence scores.

4

Yield Production

A Random Forest Regressor estimates expected production (units) and yield per hectare for your farm area.
