⚙️ How It Works

Six steps from your farm data to an accurate crop recommendation.

📥

1. Input Your Data

Enter climate conditions, farm area, state, district and season.

🔧

2. Feature Engineering

25 features are computed: Yield, log transforms, climate interactions, regional medians.

🌲

3. Random Forest

150 decision trees vote on the best crop. Majority wins.

📊

4. Probabilities

Confidence scores for all 77 crops are ranked and the top 3 shown.

📦

5. Production Estimate

A Random Forest Regressor predicts expected production in units.

✅

6. Recommendation

You get the best crop, top 3 alternatives, and full input summary.
